EverGame

EverGame is an app dedicated for tracking release dates of video games. Users can create their own personal list of games to track using their account.

Winds of Trade

Trade goods around the world! Winds of Trade is a browser game that has the player assume control of a trading vessel in the 1800s traveling through various ports.

The Social Club

Social Club is an application that allows users to create social groups according to their interests and form communities utilizing a social network-type posting and chat feature.

Vivillon Match Game

A Pokemon XY themed match game where the goal is to click on every unique pattern without repeating.

Word Guess Game

An Overlord themed Hangman style game where you guess the correct word letter by letter.

/r/worldnews Scraper

A web app that lets users view and leave comments on the top posted news from r/worldnews on reddit. Uses Mongoose and Cheerio to scrape posts from that site.
