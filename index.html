About Me

My name is Jeff Daquioag and I am currently working as a Rework Technician for Apple Computer, a FoodJets pilot, and a student at UC Davis Extension's Coding Boot Camp. I amd a graduate at UC Riverside have a Bachelor's in International Relations and an Associate's in Computer Science.

My hobbies and interests include video games, coding, and building computers. Since I began studying Computer Science, I have started many of my own projects outside of class, as well as attempting to contribute to various open source projects (in a very limited sense as I'm no expert). Personal projects include a "Pokemon"-style battle game in C++, and a sidescroller web game using Javascript. On the web development side I've created a somewhat interactive website on how to play Chess as a final class project. I also maintain a simple website with VN walkthroughs using pretty much only HTML.

It seems that pursuing a career in programming and/or development is the most suitable course for me, isn't it?

Outside of computers and video games, I enjoy playing basketball. I'm also a huge professional basketball and football fan.
